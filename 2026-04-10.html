Compliance Reinforces

FCA PS25/22 Targeted Support Goes Live — AI Guidance Framework Now Active in UK

The FCA's new "targeted support" regime came into force April 6, 2026, creating the first compliant pathway for AI-driven personalised financial guidance in the UK. Under PS25/22, authorised firms can use limited consumer data to assign individuals to predefined segments and deliver "ready-made suggestions" — without triggering the full suitability assessment required for regulated advice. The framework constrains AI strictly to segment assignment and hard-coded outputs, with mandatory disclosures. This matters because it establishes the regulatory template for how AI can operate inside the personal finance advice gap — and opens a new market for infrastructure that can certifiably operate within these guardrails. Firms began applying for targeted support permissions from March 2, 2026; expect a wave of product launches through the rest of the year.
