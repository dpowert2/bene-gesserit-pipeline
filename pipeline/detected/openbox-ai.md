## One-liner
OpenBox AI is an enterprise AI trust platform that provides Identity Security Posture Management (ISPM), Agent Lifecycle Management, and a real-time Agent Access Gateway — stopping unauthorised agentic activity before it executes.

## Thesis
OpenBox's two differentiating capabilities — cognitive behavior analysis and dynamic agent risk scoring — are designed specifically to catch emergent failure modes that static rule-based governance cannot detect. This is the gap between the compliance checkbox and real-time agentic control. As you build the regulated infrastructure layer, OpenBox could serve as the runtime guardrail partner, validating that agents operating on your platform are behaving within policy at the point of execution. The Accenture FinTech Innovation Lab placement accelerates its enterprise finserv pipeline significantly.
