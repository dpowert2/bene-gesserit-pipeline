# Sapiom

**URL:** https://www.sapiom.ai
**Gap:** Agent Authentication & Security
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** USA (San Francisco, CA)
**Score:** 77/100

## One-liner
Sapiom is building the financial infrastructure layer — identity, authentication, and micro-payment rails — that lets AI agents autonomously purchase software, APIs, and compute resources without human intervention.

## FinServ Signal
Sapiom is backed by Accel (lead), Okta Ventures, Gradient Ventures (Google), Anthropic, and Coinbase Ventures — an investor syndicate that collectively owns the authentication, identity, and crypto-payment stack. Founder Ilan Zerbib was Shopify's director of engineering for payments for five years, bringing deep financial infrastructure credibility. The company has already attracted enterprise customers who are embedding Sapiom's financial layer into production AI agent deployments.

## Team
Founded by Ilan Zerbib (ex-Shopify Director of Engineering for Payments). Backed by Accel (lead), Okta Ventures, Gradient Ventures, Array Ventures, Menlo Ventures, Anthropic, and Coinbase Ventures. Total raised: $15.75M seed.

## Thesis
Sapiom directly maps onto the "Agent Authentication & Security" gap in your framework. The core thesis — that you want to be the regulated infrastructure layer agents connect to — requires that agents can be identified, authenticated, and given financial access in a compliant way. Sapiom is building exactly that stack for the commercial internet; the open question is whether they will build a regulated, compliance-grade version for financial services or whether that vertical remains open. This gives you a potential partner for the agent-facing authentication and micro-payment capability, or a competitor to watch closely as they move upmarket from developer tooling toward enterprise financial deployments. The Coinbase Ventures and Anthropic backing is a strong signal that crypto-native and AI-native agent payments will converge.
