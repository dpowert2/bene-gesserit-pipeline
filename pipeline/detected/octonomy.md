## One-liner
Octonomy orchestrates specialised AI agents to automate complex enterprise service workflows — ERP queries, maintenance logs, technical support — with 95% accuracy and sub-20-day deployments.

## FinServ Signal
While primarily focused on heavy equipment and manufacturing, Octonomy's core capability — orchestrating multiple AI agents across heterogeneous enterprise data sources without hallucinations — is directly applicable to financial back-office workflows (loan servicing, document processing, multi-system reconciliation). Macquarie Capital as lead investor provides direct financial services DNA in the cap table.

## Team
Founded in early 2024. Backed by Macquarie Capital (lead), Capnamic, NRW.Bank, and TechVision Fund. Total funding $25M. 15-month-old company that has already raised two rounds.

## Thesis
Octonomy's "orchestration over hallucination" technical approach — specialised sub-agents coordinating on complex, unstructured enterprise data — is the same architecture financial services firms need for agentic compliance review, document underwriting, and multi-system reconciliation. Macquarie's backing gives this legitimacy in the institutional finance world. This gives you a signal that the agent orchestration architecture is maturing; Octonomy is worth monitoring for potential partnership or competitive overlap as it expands from industrial into financial services verticals.
