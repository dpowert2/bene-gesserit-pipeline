## Thesis
Armilla creates the liability layer for agent operations. As agents execute trades, process payments, and make credit decisions, they create error exposure. Insurance is infrastructure: lenders and asset managers will require it before deploying agents at scale. New category, growing TAM, strategic fit with regulated operations.
