## One-liner
Obin AI builds a production-ready agentic workforce for financial institutions, automating complex, high-volume workflows end-to-end inside a firm's controls and audit boundaries.

## FinServ Signal
Obin AI has already secured engagements with several of the world's largest financial institutions representing over $1 trillion in AUM. In production deployments, the company processed 50,000+ annual loan notices for a $500bn+ private credit fund, saving 70–90% of time while improving accuracy. Pilots are moving to production in weeks, not months. Backed by Motive Partners, a specialist fintech-focused PE and VC firm.

## Team
Co-founder and CEO Apoorv Saxena was Head of AI at JPMorgan and previously ran multiple Google Cloud AI products (Translate, Contact Center AI). Co-founder and CTO Dr. Valliappa ("Lak") Lakshmanan is a former Google and Silver Lake executive, author of seven books on applied AI, and contributor to foundational AI research. $7M seed round led by Motive Partners (March 2026).

## Thesis
Obin AI is building exactly what you need to understand: a regulated-institution-native agent layer that encodes compliance logic, produces traceable outputs, and deploys inside existing governance boundaries. Their private credit fund use case (loan notice processing at scale) is a direct proof point that agentic workflows can clear financial services' audit and accuracy bar. This gives you a potential partner or competitive benchmark as you define the regulated infrastructure layer — Obin shows the "agentic workforce" framing resonates with institutions and that FinServ-specific controls (audit trails, inspectability) are table stakes for adoption.
