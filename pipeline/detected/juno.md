# Juno

**URL:** https://juno.tax
**Gap:** Tax Optimisation
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** San Diego, CA
**Score:** 76/100

## One-liner
AI tax preparation platform for CPA firms that automates 90% of data entry across 90+ document types, reducing a 2-3 hour process to 7-10 minutes while keeping human accountants in control.

## FinServ Signal
Mid-seven-figure ARR within eight months of launch, priced at ~$45 per return. Serves small-to-medium accounting firms — a highly regulated, compliance-sensitive segment. CPA founder with practitioner experience means the product reflects real regulatory constraints rather than consumer-facing assumptions. The human-in-the-loop architecture is designed specifically for IRS compliance requirements.

## Team
Dave Haase (founder, CPA) — ran his own accounting firm in the San Francisco Bay Area before founding Juno in 2023. Backed by Bonfire Ventures (lead), Impression Ventures, and Xfund.

## Thesis
Juno sits in the growing wedge between AI tax agents (like Perplexity's tax draft tool, which is consumer-grade) and the professional compliance layer CPA firms rely on. As AI agents increasingly touch tax workflows, the infrastructure that keeps those agents within regulatory guardrails — with audit trails, human review checkpoints, and IRS-grade accuracy — becomes critical. This is a potential partner for building the regulated AI layer within professional accounting workflows: you could position your infrastructure as the compliance backbone that tools like Juno connect to for identity, payment, and regulatory reporting.
