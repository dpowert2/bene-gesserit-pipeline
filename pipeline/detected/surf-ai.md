## One-liner
Surf AI is an agentic security operations platform that ingests signals from identity, cloud, HR, IT, and data tools to build a context graph, prioritize risks by business impact, and coordinate remediation through AI-driven workflows — all while maintaining full human oversight and auditability.

## FinServ Signal
Financial institutions are among the highest-value targets for the security vulnerabilities Surf addresses: fragmented identity data, siloed security signals, and inability to act on risk in real time without adding headcount. The platform's emphasis on auditability, blast-radius limits, and human approval gates maps directly to what regulated firms need when deploying agentic security tooling.

## Team
Founded by Yair Grindlinger, Elad Horn, Roie Cohen Duwek, Avner Gideoni, and Brenton Gumucio — seasoned leaders from the Israeli cybersecurity ecosystem. Backed by Accel (lead), Cyberstarts, and Boldstart Ventures. Raised $57M at launch (March 17, 2026).

## Thesis
As banks and financial institutions begin deploying agentic AI in production, the attack surface for non-human identities and autonomous agents explodes. Surf AI addresses the foundational question of how you govern, audit, and remediate agent-driven security decisions at scale. This gives you a potential partner in the guardrails layer — a firm already thinking about agent governance architecture in regulated environments where audit trails and explainability are non-negotiable.
