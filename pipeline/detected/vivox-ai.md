## Thesis
Vivox occupies a narrow but extremely defensible position: compliance agents built specifically to satisfy regulators, not just automate compliance analysts. The "atomic agent" architecture — where each agent handles a single auditable task — mirrors the accountability and explainability requirements that financial regulators are beginning to formalise. This gives you a potential intelligence partner or acquisition target in the compliance agent layer: a UK-anchored team building exactly the kind of regulator-first architecture that enterprise financial institutions need to deploy AI in production. Their AML/KYC focus aligns with the identity and onboarding gap you're monitoring.
