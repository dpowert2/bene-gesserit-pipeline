# Certivo

**URL:** https://certivo.io
**Gap:** Compliance
**Stage:** Seed ($6M total funding)
**Geo:** Seattle, WA, USA
**Score:** 50

## One-liner
Certivo is an AI-native compliance automation platform that deploys an AI agent (CORA) to continuously monitor regulatory changes, validate supplier documentation, and map obligations to product portfolios for manufacturers and supply chain operators.

## FinServ Signal
Certivo's platform is supply-chain focused rather than directly financial-services-facing, but its "AI-native compliance automation" positioning and CORA agent architecture are directly analogous to what regulated financial institutions need for regulatory change management. Suffolk Technologies (the lead investor) has strong fintech and built-world crossover positioning in Boston. The $4M seed is early but the pattern — purpose-built AI agent replacing manual compliance operations — mirrors finserv compliance automation demand.

## Team
Spun out of Pioneer Square Labs (PSL) in 2024. Investors: Suffolk Technologies (lead), Pioneer Square Labs, Pioneer Square Ventures.

## Thesis
Certivo demonstrates that "AI-native compliance automation" is emerging as a distinct product category outside finserv, with the same architecture you need inside it: a continuously running agent that monitors regulatory changes, maps them to internal obligations, and automates documentation workflows. This gives you a pattern to watch — if Certivo or analogues pivot into financial regulatory change management (which processes like PFAS/ESG disclosures are bringing closer to banking compliance), it could become a direct competitor or acquisition target in your Compliance gap.
