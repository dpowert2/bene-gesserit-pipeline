# Numos

**Gap:** Personal Agent
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** San Francisco, CA

## One-liner
Numos is an agentic AI finance platform that sits on top of a company's existing finance stack and automates FP&A, reconciliation, and quote-to-cash workflows for enterprise CFOs.

## FinServ Signal
Enterprise customers include Udemy (public company) and Dandy (scaled private company). Platform reports 80% faster financial planning and analysis cycles and companies closing books in less than half the time. Explicitly designed to make every reasoning step auditable — critical in regulated reporting environments. Backed by General Catalyst, which has deep enterprise finance software DNA.

## Team
Founded by Parijat Sarkar and Mitul Tiwari, with backgrounds spanning finance, data engineering, and enterprise AI. Operator Collective also participated in the seed round.

## Thesis
Numos targets the CFO layer — the exact governance and control centre that enterprise agents will have to report through to get actions approved. As agentic workflows proliferate, you need trusted infrastructure that audits, reconciles, and signs off on autonomous decisions. Numos's "show your reasoning" architecture directly addresses the explainability gap that regulators are beginning to formalise. This gives you a potential partner or early acquisition target in the enterprise finance agent control layer.
