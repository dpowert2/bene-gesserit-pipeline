## One-liner
Workload identity and just-in-time access management for AI agents with automatic credential rotation.

## FinServ Signal
Aembit's backing from Ballistic Ventures, Okta Ventures, and CrowdStrike Falcon Fund signals enterprise/security pedigree. Positioning as "identity layer for non-human workloads" directly addresses agent authentication. JIT access and credential rotation are critical for regulated financial workflows where agent permissions must be granular and auditable.

## Team
Security-focused founding team with backing from identity and cybersecurity VCs (Okta, CrowdStrike). Team depth in IAM/security is strong for stage.

## Thesis
Aembit solves a narrower but equally critical problem: ephemeral, task-scoped credentials for agents. In regulated finance, agents cannot carry persistent credentials — they must request access per task, be granted it, and have it revoked immediately after. Aembit is infrastructure other agents depend on.
