# Goodfire

## One-liner
Goodfire is an AI interpretability platform that lets enterprises understand, debug, and intentionally design AI models by identifying and modifying the internal components responsible for specific model behaviours.

## FinServ Signal
AI interpretability is transitioning from academic interest to a hard compliance requirement as the EU AI Act's high-risk AI rules take effect in August 2026 — including creditworthiness scoring systems. Regulators across the FCA, Fed, and OCC are signalling that audit trails and explainability will be mandatory for financial AI deployments. Salesforce Ventures participated in the Series B round, connecting Goodfire into the enterprise finserv workflow stack.

## Team
Founded by Eric Moran and team. Investors: B Capital (lead), Juniper Ventures, Menlo Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, South Park Commons, Wing Venture Capital, DFJ Growth, Salesforce Ventures, Eric Schmidt, and others.

## Thesis
This gives you a potential infrastructure partnership for the "explainability layer" that financial regulators are converging on requiring. As you build the regulated infrastructure layer for agentic AI, you need AI systems that can demonstrate to regulators *why* a decision was made. Goodfire's model design environment — which lets users reach inside models and identify the parts responsible for specific behaviours — is precisely the kind of tooling that will underpin regulatory compliance for AI in lending, AML, and underwriting. At $1.25B valuation it is a tier-1 buy/build/partner candidate for any institution serious about AI governance.
