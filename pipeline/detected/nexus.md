# Nexus

**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** Brussels, Belgium (expanding to US)

## One-liner
Nexus enables non-technical enterprise teams to deploy autonomous AI agents across 4,000+ business tools without coding or IT support.

## FinServ Signal
Early traction with Orange and similar enterprise clients demonstrates production-grade deployment capability. Nexus positions its AI agents around measurable operational and revenue outcomes — language that resonates with compliance-heavy enterprise buyers in financial services. General Catalyst's participation signals institutional confidence in the enterprise go-to-market.

## Team
Founded in 2024 by CEO Assem Chammah and AI engineer Shady Al Shoha. Backed by General Catalyst (lead), Y Combinator, Transpose Platform, Twenty Two Ventures, Phosphor Capital, plus angels including Gokul Rajaram (former Google/Facebook/DoorDash) and Raphael Schaad.

## Thesis
Nexus is building the deployment and orchestration layer that sits between AI models and enterprise business systems — precisely the kind of integration infrastructure that financial services firms will need as agentic AI moves from pilots to production. This gives you a potential channel partner or integration target: firms adopting Nexus for internal finance workflows will need the regulated infrastructure layer underneath. The European base and General Catalyst backing make Nexus a credible enterprise-first play worth tracking as agentic AI scales into regulated industries.
