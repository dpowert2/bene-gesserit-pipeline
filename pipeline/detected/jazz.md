## One-liner
Jazz is an AI-native data loss prevention (DLP) platform that deploys an autonomous "Agentic Investigator" to understand the context and intent behind every data-related event, dramatically reducing alert noise in regulated enterprise environments.

## FinServ Signal
Jazz has MassMutual Ventures as an investor — signaling direct financial services demand. Its clients include AlphaSense (financial intelligence platform) and Lemonade (InsurTech). Their platform is live across dozens of organisations. The core product addresses a critical pain point for financial institutions: as AI agents access and move sensitive financial data, traditional DLP rules-based systems generate unmanageable alert volumes. Jazz demonstrated reduction from tens of thousands of daily false positives to ~10 pre-investigated incidents for a 5,000-person deployment.

## Team
Investors: Glilot Capital Partners (lead), Team8 (Israeli deep-tech cybersecurity VC), Ten Eleven Ventures, Merlin Ventures, Encoded Ventures, MassMutual Ventures. Strong Israeli cybersecurity founding DNA. Total raised: $61M (seed + Series A combined).

## Thesis
As you architect the regulated infrastructure layer for agentic AI in financial services, data exfiltration and misuse by AI agents is one of the highest-risk attack surfaces. Jazz's "Agentic Investigator" concept is a direct complement to your thesis: financial institutions adopting AI agents need intelligent data observability that understands agent context, not just pattern-matching rules. This gives you a potential integration partner for any audit, compliance monitoring, or agent activity logging capabilities you build — and their MassMutual Ventures backing signals they're already finding financial services traction.
