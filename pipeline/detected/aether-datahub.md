## One-liner
Aether DataHub creates human-verified, institutional-grade financial datasets for training and fine-tuning AI models in regulated financial services contexts.

## FinServ Signal
Partnering with OORT to address the acute shortage of high-quality domain-specific financial training data, Aether positions itself at the intersection of AI infrastructure and financial services data quality. Financial services firms have been blocked from using generic AI training data by regulatory concerns; Aether's verifiable datasets are explicitly designed to satisfy those concerns. Partnership announced April 1, 2026.

## Team
Limited public information available. Aether Holdings partnership with OORT (a distributed compute and data verification network) suggests an infrastructure-layer approach rather than a direct application layer.

## Thesis
Every AI agent deployed in financial services needs to be trained on accurate, compliant, and auditable data. Aether is betting that the bottleneck to agentic AI adoption in finance is not model capability but data quality. This gives you a potential data infrastructure partner: if you are building the operating system that financial agents plug into, Aether could supply the training and evaluation datasets that make those agents trustworthy and regulatorily defensible. Watch for whether they develop financial-domain benchmarks or become a data supplier to the regtech and compliance agent market.
