# Worth

**URL:** https://www.worthai.com
**Gap:** Compliance
**Stage:** Series A
**Geo:** New York, USA
**Score:** 82

## One-liner
Worth is an AI-powered onboarding and underwriting platform for financial institutions that unifies KYC, KYB, credit scoring, fraud detection, and agentic decision workflows into a real-time engine for SMB financial services.

## FinServ Signal
Worth is directly deployed by banks, fintechs, and payment providers to replace slow, fragmented SMB underwriting that can take days or weeks. Their platform uses verified identity data, network intelligence, risk signals, and ML models to generate decisions in real time. Investors include American Express Ventures and TTV Capital — two of the most credible finserv-specialist backers in the market. The company is also building a "Know Your Agent" (KYA) framework, explicitly anticipating the need to verify AI agents transacting on behalf of businesses — a forward-looking indicator of deep regulatory awareness.

## Team
Founded 2023 by Sal Rehmetullah and Suneera Madhani, both fintech industry veterans. Series A led by Fulcrum Equity Partners, with Amex Ventures and TTV Capital participating.

## Thesis
Worth's move to build a "Know Your Agent" framework is the most strategically interesting signal here. As AI agents become parties to financial transactions, the KYC/KYB paradigm must extend to agent identity and intent verification. This gives you a potential partner for discussions around agent authentication infrastructure: Worth needs a trustworthy layer to verify agent credentials, and that's exactly the gap you can position to fill. Their SMB onboarding focus also makes them an integration point for any regulated financial OS layer targeting the long tail of small business banking.
