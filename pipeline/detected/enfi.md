## One-liner
EnFi deploys AI credit analyst agents directly inside banks to automate end-to-end commercial lending workflows — reviewing borrower leverage, collateral, credit history, and documentation inconsistencies alongside human underwriters.

## FinServ Signal
EnFi is purpose-built for banks, with investors (FINTOP, Patriot Financial Partners, Commerce Ventures) collectively working with 150+ financial institutions. Their agents are deployed inside bank credit teams as virtual co-workers, operational within 60–90 days, and designed to expand lending capacity without adding headcount. They target the $112B annual credit labor market in the US, where tens of thousands of credit analyst positions remain unfilled. Total raised: $22.5M.

## Team
Boston-based. Series A led by FINTOP Capital, with Patriot Financial Partners, Commerce Ventures, Unusual Ventures, and Boston Seed Capital participating. Deep finserv VC network with direct bank relationships.

## Thesis
EnFi is a direct embodiment of the "AI agent as bank employee" thesis — which is the exact deployment model that creates demand for the regulated infrastructure layer you want to provide. As banks onboard AI credit agents at scale, they'll need compliant data pipelines, audit trails, model governance, and agent identity controls. EnFi gives you a concrete customer archetype to design around: banks running AI credit agents that need trustworthy orchestration infrastructure, not just another AI model vendor.
