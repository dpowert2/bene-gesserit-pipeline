## One-liner
imper.ai is a workforce identity security platform that detects AI-driven impersonation and social engineering attacks in real time, using dynamic contextual verification to confirm that the human behind an enterprise session is who they claim to be.

## FinServ Signal
imper.ai is already deployed with customers in financial services, wholesale, and technology. Financial services is an explicit first-mover vertical, given the high-value targets and regulatory requirements around identity verification and access control. The platform addresses the gap where attackers bypass authentication entirely through social engineering and AI-generated impersonation—a threat vector growing rapidly as autonomous agents gain access to financial systems.

## Team
- Founded by veterans of Israeli intelligence Unit 8200 (elite cyber intelligence). Backed by **Redpoint Ventures** and **Battery Ventures**. Launched publicly at RSAC 2026 (March 24-26). $28M raised as of December 2025.

## Thesis
As your strategy relies on being the trusted regulated infrastructure layer for AI agents in financial services, identity assurance at the human-agent boundary is critical. imper.ai addresses the exact failure mode that could undermine your stack: an attacker using AI to impersonate a human approver or agent operator within a financial workflow. This gives you a potential security partner for the human-in-the-loop verification layer in high-stakes agentic transactions—particularly relevant as regulators demand non-repudiation for AI-assisted financial decisions.
