## One-liner
Qevlar AI builds an autonomous AI Security Operations Centre (SOC) platform that enriches, correlates, and investigates security signals end-to-end — without human analysts — surfacing root-cause intelligence rather than just alert counts.

## FinServ Signal
Qevlar AI's customer base includes Fortune 500 enterprises such as Mercedes-Benz and Sodexo, as well as leading MSSPs including Orange Cyberdefense, ECI, and Atos — the kinds of regulated-industry managed security providers that serve major banks and insurers. Its move beyond alert triage toward organisation-level security insights directly addresses the compliance-reporting requirements of financial services firms. The $30M Series A was co-led by Partech and Forgepoint Capital International (a specialist cybersecurity VC), signalling serious regulated-market intent.

## Team
Founded in 2023. Series A co-led by Partech and Forgepoint Capital International (returning investor), with EQT Ventures participating. Forgepoint is a specialist cybersecurity investor with a track record in regulated industries.

## Thesis
As agentic AI workflows proliferate inside financial services — running compliance checks, processing loan applications, executing transactions — the attack surface of non-human identities explodes. Qevlar's autonomous SOC is positioned to monitor and investigate agentic activity at machine speed, which is a prerequisite for any trust-layer infrastructure you build. Its European base and MSSP distribution channel gives you a meaningful entry point into EU financial services buyers navigating AI Act compliance obligations simultaneously.
