# Kaaj
**One-liner:** Agentic AI credit intelligence platform that deploys coordinated AI agents to automate end-to-end small business loan underwriting — reducing credit analysis from days to under three minutes.
**Gap category:** Personal Agent
**Stage:** Seed ($3.8M, Nov 2025, Kindred Ventures lead)
**Geography:** USA
**FinServ signal:** Directly in financial services: lenders and brokers as customers, $5B+ in loan applications already processed. Clients include Amur Equipment Finance, Quality Equipment Finance, Fundr.
**Team signal:** Utsav Shah (CEO, decade at Uber and Cruise building AI decision systems); Shivi Sharma (President, ex-American Express, Uber, Varo Bank — credit and fraud risk expert). Female co-founded.
**Product maturity:** Live product (processing $5B+ loan volume, growing customer base)
**Business model:** SaaS (sold to lenders/brokers, not borrowers)

## Why This Matters
Kaaj demonstrates that agentic AI can run regulated credit workflows end-to-end with real volume ($5B+) at seed stage — a direct proof point for the regulated infrastructure thesis applied to lending. The team pedigree (Uber AI + Varo Bank) bridges consumer fintech credibility with institutional finance.

## Why You Might Pass
Kaaj serves lenders, not individual investors — the gap preference is for agents that face the end investor or advisor directly. Also at $3.8M seed with SMB lending focus, the market ceiling may feel limited compared to the broader agentic infrastructure plays elsewhere in the pipeline.
