# Lema AI

**URL:** https://www.lema.ai
**Gap:** Compliance
**Stage:** Series A
**Geo:** Tel Aviv / New York
**Score:** 78

## One-liner
Lema AI is an agentic security platform that continuously maps and evaluates third-party vendor risk using autonomous agents trained to think like vulnerability researchers — replacing static questionnaire-based compliance with real-time attack path analysis.

## FinServ Signal
Already counts financial services and healthcare companies as customers, along with multiple Fortune 500s. The platform addresses a pain point that is particularly acute for financial institutions, which rely on thousands of third-party vendors under strict regulatory expectations (OCC third-party guidance, DORA in Europe). Led by Team8, a Tel Aviv-based venture group with deep ties to financial services cybersecurity.

## Team
Founded in 2023 by Eddie Dovzhik, Omer Yehudai, and Tomer Roizman. $24M Series A led by Team8, with participation from Salesforce Ventures. Previously backed at seed by F2 Venture Capital.

## Thesis
Third-party vendor risk is one of the highest-friction compliance burdens in financial services — and it scales dangerously as firms add more AI vendors with privileged access to sensitive data. Lema's agentic approach (continuous monitoring vs. annual questionnaire) maps directly onto what regulators now expect for vendor oversight under frameworks like DORA and OCC guidance. This gives you a clear signal in the AI-powered compliance infrastructure space: the vendor risk layer is being attacked by well-funded startups. As you evaluate your own regulated infrastructure positioning, Lema is a potential partner for surfacing AI-vendor risk posture — or a benchmark for the level of agentic rigor regulators will eventually expect across all compliance workflows.
