## One-liner
AI-native financial modeling platform that replaces static planning cycles with continuously updated, queryable models — built by Klarna's former FP&A leads.

## FinServ Signal
Founded by three former Klarna employees who managed financial planning across 26 markets during the company's hypergrowth period. The platform is purpose-built for fast-growing organisations with complex, multi-market financial operations — exactly the profile of scaled fintechs and financial services firms facing planning fragmentation.

## Team
Evan Rumpza (CEO), Mattia Scolari, and Giovanni Casula — all former Klarna. Round led by J12 Ventures, with participation from Antler and angels from Klarna, DeepL, Stripe, and Plata.
