## One-liner
AI compliance agents that embed directly into enterprise workflows to review content, flag policy violations, and provide real-time approvals — eliminating the manual review bottleneck as AI-generated content volumes surge.

## FinServ Signal
Compliance teams in financial services are the canonical bottleneck that Haast targets: legal and compliance staff spending 70% of their time on repetitive content review, with regulatory obligations making accuracy non-negotiable. DST Global Partners participation in the Series A signals institutional-grade investor conviction. The company reports 4.5x revenue growth YoY and 100% customer retention — metrics consistent with genuine product-market fit in regulated enterprise environments.

## Team
Kunal Vankadara (CEO), founded 2023, HQ in New York. Series A led by Peak XV Partners (formerly Sequoia India/Southeast Asia), with DST Global Partners, Airtree, Aura Ventures, and Black Sheep Capital. Total funding $17.05M.

## Thesis
As AI-generated content volumes 8-10x inside financial institutions — marketing materials, client communications, internal reports, trade documentation — the compliance review layer becomes a critical chokepoint. Haast is building the autonomous compliance agent that sits between content creation and publication. This directly aligns with the regulated infrastructure thesis: you want to be the layer that ensures AI agents in financial services operate within regulatory bounds. Haast could be a strategic integration partner for compliance workflows, or a model for the kind of governance tooling that institutional asset managers and broker-dealers will require as they scale agentic AI.
