## What They Do

Hybridity is a Stockholm-based AI compliance platform automating digital operational resilience obligations under DORA, NIS2, and GDPR. The platform uses agentic workflows to automate evidence collection, gap analysis, and regulatory reporting for financial institutions operating in the EU.

## Why It Matters

DORA came into full force in January 2025, creating an immediate compliance obligation for EU financial institutions that most are still navigating manually. Hybridity's agentic approach to DORA compliance automation targets a large, urgent, and underserved market — with a natural expansion path to broader financial regulation as the EU AI Act compliance deadlines approach.
