**Stage:** Series A

## One-liner
Variance builds autonomous AI agents that execute fraud, KYC/AML, and compliance investigation workflows for regulated financial institutions — reducing weeks-long investigations to minutes.

## FinServ Signal
Strong: Customers include Fortune 500 companies and regulated financial services institutions. At one of the world's largest retailers, Variance agents traced ultimate beneficial ownership chains, uncovered coordinated fraud rings, and cross-referenced sanctions and adverse media. Both co-founders spent 4+ years on Apple's Fraud Engineering, Algorithms & Risk team. Okta Ventures is an investor, signalling enterprise identity/compliance credibility.

## Team
Co-founders Karine Mellata (CEO) and Michael Lin (CTO) both come from Apple's Fraud Engineering team, with additional experience at Uber and Discord. YC W23 alumni. Backed by Ten Eleven Ventures (cybersecurity specialist fund), 645 Ventures, Y Combinator, Urban Innovation Fund, and Okta Ventures. Total raised: $26M.

## Thesis
This gives you a reference point for the compliance agent category maturing fast — Variance is exactly the purpose-built, auditable, infrastructure-adjacent product the thesis anticipates. Their full-audit-trail approach and 150+ data source integrations make them a potential partner or benchmark for compliance workflow automation. If you're building the regulated infrastructure layer, Variance is the kind of application tenant you want connecting to your platform rather than building their own rails.
