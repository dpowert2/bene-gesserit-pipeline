## One-liner
Meridian is an IDE-style agentic workspace that automates complex financial modeling and spreadsheet workflows, combining AI agents with structured auditability for finance teams.

## FinServ Signal
Early customers include teams at Decagon and OffDeal; signed $5M in contracts in December 2025 alone. Product is explicitly designed around the auditability and predictability requirements of finance teams. QED Investors (fintech-specialist VC) participated in the round alongside a16z and The General Partnership.

## Team
Founded by John Ling (CEO) with team alumni from Scale AI, Anthropic, and Goldman Sachs. $17M seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz and The General Partnership, with participation from QED Investors, FPV Ventures, and Litquidity Ventures. Post-money valuation: $100M.

## Thesis
Meridian targets the sprawling, unstructured spreadsheet layer that sits at the heart of FP&A, M&A modeling, and risk analysis inside every financial institution. As agentic AI moves into the finance office, you need infrastructure that can audit and trace agent decisions — Meridian's Cursor-style IDE approach builds that traceability in from the ground up. This gives you a lens on where the agentic finance workflow layer is being captured by startups, and a potential partner or competitive signal for how your own internal modeling workflows evolve. If Meridian lands large bank FP&A teams, it becomes part of the regulated infrastructure stack by default.
