# Zoven (formerly Zivy)

## One-liner
Zoven is an AI-native compliance platform targeting fintech fraud detection, KYC, and AML for merchant-acquiring firms, built on the pivot of Blume Ventures-backed Zivy.

## FinServ Signal
Zoven is explicitly targeting fintechs and banks that acquire merchants and require rigorous background risk checks, AML screening, and KYC compliance at onboarding. Initial conversations with Indian fintech firms are already underway. The founders have regulatory-adjacent DNA: Zivy's original B2B SaaS product involved communication workflows where compliance was a key concern.

## Team
- **Vivek Karna** and **Prashant VY** (co-founders). Previously built Zivy (Blume Ventures-backed, raised Sep 2024). Retaining the parent entity and cap table; not fundraising for now. Bengaluru-based, targeting Indian market initially.

## Thesis
Zoven addresses a specific and growing gap: as agentic AI accelerates merchant onboarding and credit decisioning for fintechs, the compliance checks underpinning those flows become mission-critical. This gives you a potential signal of how compliance-as-a-service is evolving in emerging markets—where regulators are becoming more demanding about AI-driven onboarding. A partnership or watchlist position is worth considering as Zoven scales outside India.
