## One-liner
AI-first AML/KYC compliance platform that automates investor onboarding, screening, risk assessment, and periodic reviews for fund-of-funds, family offices, and offshore trust structures.

## FinServ Signal
Manages $100 billion in investor assets on-platform; purpose-built for the institutional capital structures that break traditional compliance tools — fund-of-funds, family offices, and offshore trusts. Achieves same-day onboarding in 80% of cases regardless of investor complexity. Explicitly targets AML/KYC obligations across document collection, entity screening, risk scoring, and ongoing review cycles for regulated investment managers. Backed by Motive Partners, a specialist fintech growth equity firm.

## Team
Dual-headquartered in New York and London. Seed round led by Motive Partners, with Outward VC and Cooley participating. Angel investors include Shai Wininger (co-founder, Lemonade and Fiverr), Tom Keiser (former COO of Zendesk and Carta), Remy Astié and Ulric Musset (co-founders of Vauban), Mushegh Tovmasyan (founder of Zenus Bank), and Keith Grose (UK CEO, Coinbase) — an unusually high-quality fintech angel syndicate for a seed round.

## Thesis
As you build the regulated infrastructure layer for agentic AI in financial services, compliance onboarding is a chokepoint every AI agent deployment will hit. Steward gives you a potential partner — or acquisition target — for the KYC/AML agent module that regulated financial entities will demand before allowing any autonomous system to act on their behalf. The fund-of-funds and family office focus is particularly interesting: these are exactly the sophisticated investors who will want AI agents managing portfolios and executing transactions, and who need compliant onboarding to do so. Steward is pre-emptively solving the compliance on-ramp problem for agentic finance.
