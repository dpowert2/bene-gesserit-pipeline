## One-liner
Lyzr builds on-premise enterprise AI agent infrastructure, letting banks and insurers deploy full agentic systems on their own infrastructure without sending sensitive data to external cloud platforms.

## FinServ Signal
Accenture led Lyzr's $14.5M Series A+ specifically to deploy Lyzr in banking, insurance, and financial services clients through Accenture's "Project Spotlight" accelerator. Lyzr's valuation quintupled to $250M in this round, reflecting the premium placed on a compliance-grade, on-prem agent orchestration approach. The on-premise architecture is the key differentiator — it directly addresses the data sovereignty and regulatory requirements that prevent most cloud-based agent platforms from clearing procurement in regulated financial institutions.

## Team
Founded in 2023. Backed by Accenture Ventures (lead), with prior investors from the $8M Series A. Total raised: ~$22.5M+. Accenture's distribution network gives Lyzr a significant GTM advantage into regulated enterprise accounts.

## Thesis
Lyzr reinforces the thesis that regulated financial institutions will not accept cloud-hosted agent orchestration for sensitive workflows. This gives you a potential partner or component for your on-premise infrastructure offering — specifically the agent runtime and orchestration layer for banks that require data to stay within their own environment. The Accenture backing is particularly meaningful: it signals that the world's largest consulting firm is now systematically routing enterprise finserv AI agent projects through on-prem architecture rather than hyperscaler defaults. This accelerates the market timeline for your regulated-infrastructure thesis.
