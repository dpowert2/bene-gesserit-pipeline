# JetStream Security

**URL:** https://www.jetstreamsecurity.com
**Gap:** AI Safety & Guardrails
**Stage:** Seed ($34M)
**Geo:** United States
**Score:** 78

## One-liner
JetStream Security provides real-time AI governance and visibility to enterprises through "AI Blueprints" — dynamic system-generated graphs that continuously map how AI models operate across the organisation and flag policy violations before they cause harm.

## FinServ Signal
The platform's real-time compliance monitoring, audit trails for AI outputs, and access control for sensitive prompts are directly applicable to financial services firms under FINRA and SEC supervision. The involvement of Okta co-founder Frederic Kerrest as an angel suggests a strong identity/access management angle — critical for firms managing AI agent permissions within regulated workflows.

## Team
CEO Raj Rajamani was previously CrowdStrike's Chief Product Officer. Founding team draws from Attivo Networks, CrowdStrike, Cohesity, Dazz, McAfee, and SentinelOne. Investors: Redpoint Ventures (lead), CrowdStrike Falcon Fund. Angel investors include CrowdStrike CEO George Kurtz, Wiz CEO Assaf Rappaport, and Okta co-founder Frederic Kerrest.

## Thesis
JetStream addresses the same enterprise governance gap as Onyx Security but from a pedigree cybersecurity angle. This is a watch for partnership or competitive benchmarking: as you position as the regulated infrastructure layer, JetStream's AI Blueprints concept — dynamic graphs of agent behaviour — could inform the transparency and audit primitives your platform offers to institutional clients. The Okta/identity management DNA is particularly interesting for agent authentication use cases.
