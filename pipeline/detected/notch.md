## One-liner
Notch is building the AI operating system for regulated industries, deploying production-ready AI agents that automate end-to-end compliance-critical operational workflows for insurers and financial institutions.

## FinServ Signal
Originally founded in 2021 as a specialty insurer, Notch was built by practitioners who hit the wall with available AI tools being unpredictable, hard to audit, and unfit for regulated environments. Their platform is now live across leading global insurers, large brokers, and financial services firms. ARR grew 12x in the past 12 months. Investors include Illuminate Financial — a finserv-specialist VC — and Phoenix Insurance. Use cases span policy servicing, claims intake, underwriting submissions, AML document review, and back-office co-piloting for adjusters and underwriters.

## Team
Founded 2021 out of firsthand operational insurance experience. Series A led by Headline, with Lightspeed Venture Partners, Jibe Ventures, Illuminate Financial, and Phoenix Insurance participating. Total funding: $45M.

## Thesis
Notch is positioning itself almost exactly where you want to be: the regulated infrastructure layer that AI agents run on inside financial institutions. Their platform enforces auditability, traceability, and compliance controls at the workflow level — not bolted on after the fact. This gives you a potential partner or competitive benchmark for any pitch positioning you as the trusted orchestration substrate for agentic workflows in financial services. They prove the category is real and fundable; the question is whether you differentiate on the financial data layer, identity, or custody infrastructure they don't touch.
