## One-liner
Results-based billing infrastructure for AI agents charging based on outcomes rather than seats.

## FinServ Signal
Founded by Manny Medina (ex-Outreach, $4.4B exit), Paid.ai raised €10M pre-seed + $21.6M Series A (oversubscribed, Lightspeed lead). IFS signed as customer. If service-as-software is the agentic business model, Paid.ai is building the billing rails. Outcome-based pricing is essential for agent value realization.

## Team
Experienced fintech founder with successful exit. Strong venture backing (Lightspeed, major VCs). Series A stage with product traction.

## Thesis
Paid.ai solves monetization for agent service providers. If agents are packaged as services and priced on outcomes, billing infrastructure is needed. However, this is one layer removed from core regulated infrastructure (auth, payments, compliance). Application-layer more than infrastructure-layer. Good fit but not highest priority.
