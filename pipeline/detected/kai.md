# Kai

**URL:** https://www.kai.ai
**Gap:** AI Safety & Guardrails
**Stage:** Series A equivalent (emerged from stealth)
**Geo:** USA (Fort Lauderdale, FL)
**Score:** 70/100

## One-liner
Kai is the first agentic AI cybersecurity platform, using autonomous AI agents to detect, reason about, and respond to threats at machine speed across both IT and OT environments.

## FinServ Signal
Kai already counts Fortune 500 enterprises among its customers and has generated seven-figure bookings in its first 10 months. Financial institutions are a natural target market: they face the highest density of AI-driven attacks, carry the most regulated infrastructure, and increasingly rely on agent-to-agent communication that must be protected. The founders built Claroty and SecurityMatters — two companies with deep industrial and financial sector traction.

## Team
Founded by the creators of Claroty (industrial cybersecurity, acquired for ~$1.5B by Rockwell Automation) and SecurityMatters. Backed by Evolution Equity Partners (lead), N47, and strategic co-investors. $125M raised on emergence from stealth with no prior public rounds.

## Thesis
As you build the regulated infrastructure layer for agentic AI in financial services, security of agent-to-agent communication and of the infrastructure itself becomes a mandatory component. Kai represents a potential integration partner or layer — its agentic threat detection sits directly in the path of any compliance-grade deployment. A financial services-specific version of Kai's autonomous SOC capability could be a competitive advantage for any regulated agent runtime. This also signals that agentic cybersecurity is maturing into a standalone category — worth monitoring for partnership or due-diligence purposes as you develop your own safety and guardrail standards.
