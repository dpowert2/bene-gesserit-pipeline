## What They Do

Norm AI builds AI-powered regulatory compliance automation for financial institutions. Their platform encodes regulatory rules as executable policies that AI agents can interpret and enforce in real time — enabling firms to automate compliance workflows rather than relying on manual review.

## Why It Matters

As agentic AI systems take on more financial tasks, they will need machine-readable compliance guardrails embedded at the infrastructure level. Norm AI's approach of making regulation executable is a direct enabler of production agentic deployment in regulated environments.
