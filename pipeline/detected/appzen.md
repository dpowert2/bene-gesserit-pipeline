## One-liner
AppZen is the agentic AI orchestration layer for the enterprise CFO office — AI agents that connect and autonomously run finance processes (T&E, AP, procurement) across borders, languages, and ERP systems.

## FinServ Signal
Customers include Amazon, Salesforce, JP Morgan Chase, Airbus, and Databricks. $290M total raised. The company works directly within SAP, Oracle, and other ERP environments — deeply embedded in regulated enterprise finance stacks. JP Morgan Chase as a customer signals institutional financial services validation. The December 2025 Agent Studio launch lets finance teams build custom AI agents from SOPs without coding — a direct threat to BPO outsourcing in finance operations.

## Thesis
AppZen is the most enterprise-validated agentic finance company in the market — it already has JP Morgan Chase as a customer and is pitching itself as an autonomous alternative to BPO outsourcing for finance teams. The Agent Studio (December 2025) letting enterprises build custom finance agents from SOPs is a direct move into the territory where regulated infrastructure matters most. This gives you a benchmark competitor and potential integration partner: enterprises that run their finance ops on AppZen agents will need authenticated, compliant execution rails underneath. AppZen validates the market thesis while defining where the infrastructure gap sits below the application layer.
