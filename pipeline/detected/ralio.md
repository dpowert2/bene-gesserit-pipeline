## One-liner
Programmable payment infrastructure for autonomous AI agents, with built-in identity verification, spend controls, and human-in-the-loop safeguards.

## FinServ Signal
Ralio targets the exact trust gap that emerges when AI agents conduct financial transactions autonomously — a problem financial services regulators are actively flagging. The company's tokenised identity verification and programmable spend controls map directly onto the kinds of oversight requirements that FINRA and the FCA are beginning to articulate for agentic AI in financial workflows.

## Team
Founded in 2025 by Leonardo Rosales and Ghali Bennani Laafiret. Backed by SVV, Seed X, Love Ventures, Plug and Play, and Antler.

## Thesis
Ralio is building the safety layer that regulated financial institutions will require before they can deploy AI agents with payment authority. As agentic workflows expand into treasury, procurement, and accounts payable, you need infrastructure that enforces scope, logs intent, and supports auditability — precisely what Ralio offers. This gives you a potential partner or acquisition target for the "guardrails-as-a-service" layer of your agentic infrastructure stack.
