## One-liner
Harvey is an AI agent platform for legal and professional services, automating contract analysis, compliance due diligence, litigation support, and regulatory workflows at scale across law firms and enterprise legal teams.

## FinServ Signal
Harvey is deployed across 1,300+ organisations and 100,000+ lawyers, with significant traction in regulated-industry legal departments—including financial services firms. Its compliance due diligence and contract analysis capabilities are directly applicable to financial services regulatory work: ISDA documentation, AML policy review, regulatory filings, and M&A diligence. At $190M ARR (January 2026) and growing, Harvey has the market presence and enterprise trust that underpins financial services adoption. GIC (Singapore's sovereign wealth fund) led the latest round, signalling sovereign-level confidence in AI-driven legal infrastructure.

## Thesis
Harvey's scale in enterprise legal work makes it a significant player in the compliance infrastructure stack for financial services. As financial institutions increasingly rely on AI agents to handle regulatory documentation, counterparty diligence, and contract review, Harvey positions itself as a natural connector between legal workflows and the broader regulated financial operating system you are building toward.
