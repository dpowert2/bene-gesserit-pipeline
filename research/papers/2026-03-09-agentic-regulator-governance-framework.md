# The Agentic Regulator: Risks for AI in Finance and a Proposed Agent-based Framework for Governance

**Authors:** (Multiple authors, see paper)
**Key finding:** Proposes an agent-based framework for AI governance in financial services, addressing the risks of agentic AI deployment. Argues that regulators themselves should adopt agentic approaches to keep pace with AI-driven financial services — a "fight fire with fire" approach to governance.
**Thesis relevance:** Directly validates the Compliance gap and regulatory urgency. If regulators adopt agentic approaches, the compliance infrastructure for AI agents becomes even more critical. Reinforces you's position as regulated infrastructure that can bridge agent-to-regulator requirements.
