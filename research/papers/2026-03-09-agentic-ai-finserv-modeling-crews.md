# Agentic AI Systems Applied to Tasks in Financial Services: Modeling and Model Risk Management Crews

**Authors:** (Multiple authors, see paper)
**Key finding:** The paper explores agentic crew workflows with human-in-the-loop modules for financial modeling and model risk management (MRM). The modeling crew uses specialized agents — a judge agent plus agents for EDA, feature engineering, model selection, training, evaluation, and documentation — demonstrating that multi-agent collaboration can handle complex regulated financial tasks.
**Thesis relevance:** Directly validates the Agent Orchestration gap — multi-agent crews with defined roles can execute complex financial modeling workflows. Also highlights the importance of human-in-the-loop for regulated contexts, reinforcing the Safety & Guardrails gap.
**Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.05439
